Neville Longbottom

Beater

Nickname: "The Longbottom Wall"

Position: Beater

What does he mean to the team?

Neville is the soul of the team. His incredible dedication and courage inspire everyone around him. He is not always the most technical player, but his heart makes up for everything. He is the quiet defender who will never let his teammates be wronged.

His contribution and key moments:

His transformation after he stood up for Hermione showed true Gryffindor character. His key tackle in the final was the beginning of the comeback. He reminds us that true strength comes from devotion to those you love.
